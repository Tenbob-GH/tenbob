Top Tips for XRPL Newbies

Avoid FOMO

Don’t get caught in the hype. Take your time before buying — especially when everyone is screaming about a token.

Trust No One (Fully)

Even in “good” communities, always do your own research. Scams and rugs can happen anywhere.

Take Time to Find the Right Community

Don’t just join the loudest group. Look for communities that feel genuine and helpful over time.

Expect Drama & Dips

Memecoins are volatile. Expect drama in communities and big price swings. Don’t panic sell or FOMO in.

Don’t Buy Green Candles

Buying when a token is already pumping hard is one of the fastest ways to lose money. Be patient.

Only Trade Memes You Believe In

Don’t “date” memes. Only trade tokens you actually have conviction in. Emotional attachment usually leads to bad decisions.

Stay Vigilant – Scams Are Everywhere

No one will ever send you free money. If something sounds too good to be true, it almost always is a scam.

Do Your Own Research (DYOR)

Never buy a token just because someone shilled it. Check the chart, community, and token info yourself.

Start Small

Only put in what you’re okay losing while you’re still learning. Treat it like paying for experience.
